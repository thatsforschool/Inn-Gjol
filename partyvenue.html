Party Venue

We want Gjøl Kro to be a place for everyone and fill the inn with plenty of humour and laughter. We offer group bookings, à la carte in the restaurant and drinks in the bar area.

What Do We Offer

Regarding groups, between the sunroom and the main room, we can house 150 seated guests. We can hold different types of events, including parties, weddings, milestone birthdays, confirmations and much more. We of course set up the tables and decorations, and we also can help customers add a personaltouch with flowers and other decorations. We plan the menu together with our customers to match their wishes and the current season.
