Menu

The inn shouldn’t be seen as ”old school”, quite the opposite. We want to bring it up to the modern standard of culinary experiences in restaurants, also for large groups. We focus on local raw goods and our food is traditional Danish meals with a modern twist.

Lunch Menu

We invite you to Gjøl Kro where each meal introduces its own uniqueness and provide you bites of traditional Danish lunch food with modern touch to it.

Dinner Menu
